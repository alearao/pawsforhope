Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteering is a rewarding way to help animals and meet like-minded people. Tasks include walking dogs, socializing cats, cleaning, assisting at adoption events, and supporting transport. Volunteers gain training, experience, and the satisfaction of helping animals find homes.

  • Dog walking & enrichment: daily walks and playtime.
  • Cleaning & care: shelter upkeep and feeding.
  • Event support: adoption booths and outreach.
  • Transport: vet runs and pet transfers.
  • Admin & media: photos, listings, and social posts.

Time commitment: flexible ‚Äî from one-off events to weekly shifts. All volunteers receive orientation and support.
