About Paws for Hope

At Paws for Hope, our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets in need across our community. We believe every animal deserves love, care, and a forever home where they can thrive.

Our Journey

  • 2018 – Founded Paws for Hope with a small team of dedicated volunteers and a vision to transform animal rescue in our community.
  • 2019 – Established our first foster network, enabling us to care for more animals while they awaited their forever homes.
  • 2021 – Opened our new state-of-the-art shelter facility with modern veterinary care and comfortable living spaces for our rescued pets.
  • 2023 – Launched our community education programs, teaching responsible pet ownership and animal welfare awareness.
  • 2024 – Celebrated our 1,000th successful adoption, marking a major milestone in our mission to find loving homes for pets in need.
  • Today – Continuing to grow our impact with new partnerships, expanded services, and an unwavering commitment to animal welfare.
